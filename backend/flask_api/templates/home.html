Who Are We?

We aim to combat period poverty by creating opportunities to support menstrual health in underserved areas, foster global solidarity and drive social change.

We are a passionate group of Computer Engineering students at the University of Waterloo, driven by a shared mission to revolutionize menstrual health through technology. Combining our expertise in software development, community outreach, and advocacy, we are dedicated to addressing period poverty with innovative solutions. FlowForward is more than just an application—it’s a movement built on the pillars of community, education, and sustainability, ensuring everyone has access to the resources and support they deserve.

Community

Empower users by facilitating donation campaigns and connecting them with localized menstrual health resources.

Education

Break the stigma surrounding menstruation through open dialogue, peer support groups, and educational forums.

Sustainability

Advocate for eco-friendly solutions through sustainable products and trusted brands, regardless of socioeconomic status.

Services

CHECK OUR APP SERVICES

Personalized Menstrual Tracking

Users can track their symptoms, moods, and energy levels through a customizable interface. The interactive menstrual calendar allows for intuitive cycle management with optional period-syncing features.

AI-Driven Health Insights

FlowForward leverages AI to analyze cycle patterns, predict irregularities, and identify potential health concerns, empowering users to take proactive steps toward their well-being.

Routine Health Reminders

The app provides timely reminders for routine health checkups, ensuring users stay informed about their reproductive health and wellness.

Community and Support Network

Users can connect with a global community, access localized menstrual health resources, and participate in donation campaigns to support menstrual equity.

Unmatched Privacy and Security

Unlike other apps, FlowForward prioritizes user data protection through end-to-end encryption, ensuring that menstrual health tracking remains safe, private, and secure.

Sustainability and Social Impact

The platform promotes sustainable menstrual products, partners with ethical brands for discounts, and actively works to combat period poverty through advocacy and outreach.
