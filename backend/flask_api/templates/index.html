Who Are We?

We aim to combat period poverty by creating opportunities to support menstrual health in underserved areas, foster global solidarity and drive social change.

We are a passionate group of Computer Engineering students at the University of Waterloo, driven by a shared mission to revolutionize menstrual health through technology. Combining our expertise in software development, community outreach, and advocacy, we are dedicated to addressing period poverty with innovative solutions. FlowForward is more than just an application—it’s a movement built on the pillars of community, education, and sustainability, ensuring everyone has access to the resources and support they deserve.

Community

Empower users by facilitating donation campaigns and connecting them with localized menstrual health resources.

Education

Break the stigma surrounding menstruation through open dialogue, peer support groups, and educational forums.

Sustainability

Advocate for eco-friendly solutions through sustainable products and trusted brands, regardless of socioeconomic status.
